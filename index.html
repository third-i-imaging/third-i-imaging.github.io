Medical Imaging and Diagnostics

At Third i, we understand that high-quality imaging services are critical to accurately diagnosing and effectively treating your medical condition. That’s why we are committed to providing the region’s most advanced inpatient and outpatient imaging services using state-of-the-art technology. Our expert radiologists use the latest technology to accurately and efficiently diagnose, monitor and treat a range of illnesses and injuries.

Infrastructure

Philips Ingenia dSync MRI Digital 70Cm

Ingenia delivers premium image quality with digital clarity and speed –and with iPatient, it provides patient-centric imaging, from patient set-up to image result.

GE Optima 660CT FREEdom Edition 4D 500S

The Optima CT660 is designed to help you address challenging imaging situations, offering both high image quality and multiple dose reduction features on one platform.

Fuji FDR Smart f Digital Radiography

Fujifilm's latest high-quality, cost effective x-ray system. Easily Installed in limited spaces and easy to use, this user friendly system will bring upon a smart workflow for you.

Bet Medical Lilyum Mammography

The Lilyum in BYM version is fitted with an isocentric C-Arm that allows all breast projections without adjusting height of the C-Arm. The isocentric rotation eliminates the C-Arm height adjustment when doing Cranio.

EPIQ 7G Philips Untrasound & Color Doppler

EPIQ 7 features our most powerful architecture ever applied to ultrasound imaging - touching all aspects of acoustic acquisition and processing, allowing you to truly experience ultrasound's evolution to a more definitive modality.
